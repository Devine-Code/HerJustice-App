General Provisions
Additional Instruments - Each party, from time to time, at the request of the other or the legal representatives of the other, shall execute, acknowledge, and deliver to the other party any and all further instruments which may reasonably be required to give full force and effect to the provisions of this Stipulation.

Binding Effect - Except as otherwise stated herein, all the provisions of this Stipulation shall be binding upon the respective heirs, next of kin, executors and administrators of the parties.

Effective Date - This Stipulation shall become effective when the parties have exchanged original counterparts of this Stipulation bearing the notarized signature of each party.

Entire Stipulation - This Stipulation constitutes the entire understanding of the parties. There are no representations, promises, warranties, covenants or undertakings other than those expressly set forth herein.

Estate Obligations - This Stipulation shall bind and inure to the benefit of the parties hereto, their heirs, executors, administrators, representatives and assigns.

Governing Law - All matters relating to the execution, validity, interpretation and performance of this Stipulation shall be governed, construed and determined in accordance with the laws of the State of New York, regardless of the forum in which an action in law or in equity may be brought by either of the parties hereto.

Modification - No provision of this Stipulation shall be changed or modified, nor shall this Stipulation be discharged or terminated in whole or in part, except by an instrument in writing signed and acknowledged by the party against whom the change, modification, discharge or termination is claimed or sought to be enforced.

4. The parties agree that each will notify the other of any change of address and/or telephone number within five (5) days of such change.


No Waiver - The failure of either of the parties to insist, in any one or more instances, upon a strict performance of any of the covenants or provisions of this Stipulation shall not be construed as a waiver or a relinquishment for the future of such covenant or provision, but the same shall continue and remain in full force and effect.

Notices - All notices sent to the Wife shall be sent by certified mail to her address set forth on the first page of this Stipulation, or to any new place of permanent residence acquired hereafter, notice of which new residence she shall have given to the Husband. All notices sent to the Husband shall be sent by certified mail to his address set forth on the first page of this Stipulation, or to any new place of permanent residence acquired hereafter, notice of which new residence he shall have given to the Wife.

Possible Invalidity - In case any provision of this Stipulation shall be held to be contrary to, or invalid under, the law of any country, state, or other jurisdiction, such illegality or invalidity shall not affect in any way the other provisions hereof, all of which shall continue, nevertheless, in full force and effect; any provision which is held to be illegal or invalid in any country, state or other jurisdiction shall, nevertheless, remain in full force and effect in any country, state or jurisdiction in which such provision is legal and valid.

Reconciliation - This Stipulation shall not be invalidated or otherwise affected by a temporary reconciliation between the parties hereto, or a resumption of marital relations between them, unless said reconciliation or said resumption be accompanied by a written statement signed by the parties with respect to same, additionally setting forth that they are canceling this Stipulation. This Stipulation shall not be invalidated or otherwise affected by any decree or judgment of separation, annulment or divorce made by any court in this action or in any other action which may hereafter be instituted by either party against the other for a separation, annulment or divorce, and the obligations and covenants of this Stipulation shall be incorporated by a reference in and shall survive any decree or judgment or separation or divorce and shall not merge therein. The parties shall not, in any action or proceeding for divorce or similar relief either previously commenced or hereafter commenced, seek any different or additional relief than the terms set forth herein.

Signature in Counterpart - This Stipulation may be executed by separate signatures and acknowledgements of the parties on counterpart copies hereof.
